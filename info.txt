GAMEPLAY:
Player moves to the right
Timer that counts down for each room, get to the next one or you lose!

WORLD GENERATION:
Blocks of the ground are created
Different platforms are spawned on a grid-like thingy
Grid is divided into multiples of 50 for height and multiples
Make platform legs extend down to the ground or other platforms
Randomly put ladders on the side of platforms that extend to other platforms or the ground
